Teaching Philosophy

Teacher Identity

When i think of myself as an english teacher, I realize that what characterizes me the most is my patience and they way I try to understand others. I have always believed that each student needs their own to learn, because not everyone learns in the same way. That is why I like the idea of creating a calm environment and finding the appropriate method or resource so that each students, according to their needs, can learn and feel comfortable, at peace and without fear of making mistakes. I feel that one of my strengths is listening and offering support, because sometimes students do not only need explanations, but someone who motivates them to keep trying.

My way of teaching is based on consistency and making learning meaningful. I like to use resources that connect to real life, such as videos, songs or activities that make english that feel more familiar and less intimidating. I do not want my classes to be only theory; I want my students to truly use what they learn.

My goals as a teacher is to help others move forward little by little, just as I have progressed in-my own process, I would like my students to feel capable , curious , and less afraid of speaking english. More than teaching a language, I want to guide them until they find their own confidence.
