Teacher Paulina

Bachelor's Degree in foreign Languages with  an Emphasis in English

Universidad Abierta y Distancia Unad

My name is Maria Paulina Lora Pacheco. I am from Colombia, and i am currently studying a Bachelor's Degree in Foreign Languages with a Emphasis in English at UNAD. I chose this program because i have always been interested in learning other languages and how they open professional, cultural, and social opportunities. Additionally , i am motivated by the idea of becoming a teacher capable of supporting others in their learning process.

Throughout this term, i have strengthened my skills in English, especially in listening, vocabulary, and speaking. Little by little. I have gained more confidence when communicating and i have learned to be more consistent in my process, even when difficulties arise. This portafolio brings together the activities completed during the course and shows my progress, my effort, and the learning i have built. I am committed to continuing to improve an to advancing toward a high level of proficiency and communicate fluently.

In my free time, i enjoy writing, reading, and painting, which helps me maintain a balance between my studies, responsibilities, and well-being.
